3. The Shawshank Redemption (1994):Frank Darabont's adaptation of Stephen King's novella is a tale of hope and redemption set within the confines of Shawshank State Penitentiary. Andy Dufresne (Tim Robbins) and Ellis "Red" Redding (Morgan Freeman) form an enduring friendship as they navigate life behind bars. This film resonates for its themes of perseverance, friendship, and the indomitable human spirit.
